Emails

Subject: What's cooking in the sales world?
Preview: A recap of recent news, data and...


The bi-weekly article newsletters from Hunterz.io suffer from low click rates. As their customer base consists of mainly sales pros and freelancers, the articles sent were usually catered to only one persona. This led us to diagnose that the problem was irrelevancy of the articles. Hence, a new layout of featuring 3 articles (a main article and 1 article for each persona) was proposed.

However, the challenge lies in setting a layout that called out to each persona and also encourage click-through. 2 layouts were A/B tested and the winning tactic (left) was to include a preview of the article that ends with a cliff-hanger.

Involvement: Concept + Design
